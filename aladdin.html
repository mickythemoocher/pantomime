Jasmine: No, Too rich.

Emperor: (Incredulously) Too rich!! Too Rich!! My child, there is no such thing as too rich

Jasmine: If you make me marry father I shall just run away

Emperor: Run away? Don't be so ridiculous. Here you have everything that you could ever desire. The worlds finest food, the finest dresses in the land, servants to cater for your every need and reaches beyond the dream of Everest

Jasmine: I think you mean avarice. Besides, I do not want any of those things, I just want to be free

Emperor: But my sweet child you are free

Jasmine: No I am not father. I have not left this palace since the day I was born

Emperor: That is to keep you safe my dear. Out there is a dangerous world. It is full of criminals, Robbers, Thieves and worst of all – politicians

Jasmine: I will take my chances. I cannot stand to be cooped up any longer

Emperor: I implore you my dear. Never, ever think of running away. Out there is a dangerous place and A beautiful princess like yourself will always be in danger.

Jasmine: I might enjoy a bit of danger, better than being cooped up here

Emperor: Well it is your bed time now. I urge to to think very seriously about the marriage proposals you have and to accept one very quickly. By tomorrow at the latest. Goodnight my dear

Jasmine: Goodnight father, and for the last time I do not want to be married.

( The emperor leaves in a huff slamming the door behind him)

Jasmine: Whatever my father says, I will not get married and I will run away. In fact that is what I am going to do right now. And I don't want people to know I am a princess. I want to be treated like a normal person. No more servants, no more people telling me what to do. Freedom at last to be just who I want to be. I may even get a job! What fun. It will be a new life, Away from my controlling father and away from this awful palace. I will pack just a small bag. I want to live simply and freely. In fact I will start packing right now and slip out secretly while everyone else is asleep
